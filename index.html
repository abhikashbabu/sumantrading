Privacy Policy – Sumon Trading App

Last Updated: December 2, 2025

App Name: Sumon Trading App

Developer: Appsredx

Sumon Trading App respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 User Provided Information

When you submit a complaint or register as a service provider, we may collect:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number
  • Email Address
  • Full Address
  • Complaint Details
  • Service Name
  • Profile Image (optional)
  • Admin/Servicer login credentials (admin panel only)

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location (GPS & Network Based): Used only to auto-fetch the user’s current location when submitting a complaint. We do not track your location in the background.
  • Random OTP Code: The OTP is auto-generated inside the app. It is not sent to any phone number, and no SMS service is used.
  • Device Information: Basic device information may be collected for analytics and app stability.

2. Permissions We Use & Why

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
    To fetch your accurate location for complaint filing and servicer visit support.
  • CAMERA
    Used by servicers while solving a complaint to capture and upload proof images.
  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE
    To allow users to upload a profile image.
  • INTERNET
    Required for communication between the app and server, submitting complaints, assigning tasks, and notifications.

No other permissions are used beyond those required for the core functionality of the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Filing and managing complaints
  • Fetching user location for complaint and service purposes
  • Assigning complaints to servicers
  • Verifying admin and servicer login
  • Sending important notifications
  • Improving app performance and user experience

We do not share, sell, or rent your information to any third party, except as required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

4. Google Sign-In

We offer secure login using Google Sign-In. Through Google Sign-In, we only access:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Profile image (optional)

We do not access your contacts, messages, or any additional personal data from your Google account.

5. Firebase Messaging & Local Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging and local notifications to send:

  • Complaint status updates
  • Admin assignment notifications
  • Servicer task updates

These notifications are limited to service-related information and do not contain sensitive personal data.

6. Admin Panel & Servicer App

6.1 Admin Panel

The admin can:

  • Add new servicers
  • View all complaints
  • Assign specific complaints to any servicer

Admin data required:

  • Name
  • Email
  • Mobile Number
  • Password

6.2 Servicer App

The servicer can:

  • View assigned complaints
  • Update complaint status
  • Upload images using the camera as proof of work

No unnecessary personal data is collected from servicers beyond what is needed for their role.

7. Data Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard security practices and take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Modification
  • Misuse or loss
  • Theft or disclosure

8. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Data Retention

We retain complaint and user data only as long as necessary for service operations and legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account or complaint data, subject to any legal requirements.

10. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication (for Google Sign-In)
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • URL Launcher (for opening web links)

These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

  • Request correction of your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request deactivation of your account
